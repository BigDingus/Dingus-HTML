About Schierke:

- Young witch apprentice to mistress Flora, met with Guts' and his travelling companions, when they were beckoned to a meeting with the powerful witch Flora.
After an attack on Flora's sanctuary, Schierke has been recognized by Flora as a fully-fledged witch, advised to help Guts in his quest against the inhumans.
