Guts

About Guts:

renowned as the "Black Swordsman", is a former mercenary and branded wanderer who travels the world in a constant internal struggle between pursuing his own ends and protecting those dear to him.

"You’re right, we are mortal and fragile.
But even if we are tortured or wounded, we’ll fight to survive.
You should feel the pain we feel and understand.
I am the messenger that will deliver you to that pain and understanding.”
-Guts

Friends:

  • Casca

    - Guts' romantic interest and longtime companion. She has survived the horrors of the Eclipse alongside Guts,
    but bears severe mental scarring since then, going as far developping a simple-minded, non-verbal alter-ego.

  • Rickert

    - Tinkerer and a former brother-in-arms. Rickert was the youngest member of the Band of the Hawk, who wasn't present during the Eclipse, thus being left in the dark for the most part.
    After Godo's passing, he took care of Godo's adoptive daughter, Erica, and headed towards the newly founded city of Fantasia.

  • Puck

    - Guts' sidekick. Very little is known about Puck, other than him being from Elfheim.

  • Isidro

    - A young vagabond, following Guts in hopes of learning to fight like him. Like Puck, not much is known about his life before meeting Guts and company, when and why he started travelling on his own.

  • Schierke

    - Witch and a port in the storm for Guts during combat. A former witch-in-training, now fully recognized as a witch before her master's passing. Now she's taken Farnese under her wing and provides information about magic to the group.

  • Serpico

    - Former Holy See, a loyal bodyguard to his, initially estranged sister, Lady Farnese.

  • Farnese

    - Former Holy See, now witch apprentice under Schierke.
