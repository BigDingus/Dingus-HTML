Puck

About Puck:

Not much is known of Puck prior to his run-in with Guts, other than coming from Elfheim, but ever since then, he sticked by Guts through everything they went through. A good summary of Puck might be that he's a man(elf?)-child, comedic and loyal.

Friends:

  • Guts

    - former unit commander of the Band of the Hawk.

  • Isidro

    - A young vagabond, following Guts in hopes of learning to fight like him. Like Puck, not much is known about his life before meeting Guts and company, when and why he started travelling on his own.

  • Schierke

    - Witch and a port in the storm for Guts during combat. A former witch-in-training, now fully recognized as a witch before her master's passing. Now she's taken Farnese under her wing and provides information about magic to the group.

  • Serpico

    - Former Holy See, a loyal bodyguard to his, initially estranged sister, Lady Farnese. He was raised by his poor, single mother, whom he had to start taking care of and as such has wound up working for his sister as a butler, albeit unknowing of their relation at first. He hasn't left her side since.

  • Farnese

    - Former Holy See, now witch apprentice under Schierke.
