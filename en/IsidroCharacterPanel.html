Isidro

About Isidro:

A young rascal, who the group has run into on their quest to save Casca. He's a self-interested, arrogant, but resourceful aswell, and has helped the group many times over their journey.

Friends:

  • Casca

  • Serpico

    - Former Holy See, a loyal bodyguard to his, initially estranged sister, Lady Farnese.

  • Farnese

    - Former Holy See, now witch apprentice under Schierke.
