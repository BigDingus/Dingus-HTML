Serpico

About Serpico:

He was raised by his poor, single mother, whom he had to start taking care of and as such has wound up working for his sister as a butler, albeit unknowing of their relation at first. He hasn't left her side since.

Friends:

  • Farnese

    - Serpico's blood related sister, former Holy See army commander, and now witch apprentice under Schierke.

  • Guts

    - former unit commander of the Band of the Hawk.

  • Casca

    - Former, second unit commander of the Band of the Hawk, who's helped by Schierke and the rest of the group recover her memory and in doing so, her old self.

  • Puck

    - Guts' sidekick. Very little is known about Puck, other than him being from Elfheim.

  • Schierke

    - Witch and a port in the storm for Guts during combat. A former witch-in-training, now fully recognized as a witch before her master's passing. Now she's taken Farnese under her wing and provides information about magic to the group.

  • Isidro

    - A young vagabond, following Guts in hopes of learning to fight like him. Like Puck, not much is known about his life before meeting Guts and company, when and why he started travelling on his own.
