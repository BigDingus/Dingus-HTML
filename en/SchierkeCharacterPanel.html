Schierke

About Schierke:

Young witch apprentice to mistress Flora, met with Guts' and his travelling companions, when they were beckoned to a meeting with the powerful witch Flora. After an attack on Flora's sanctuary, Schierke has been recognized by Flora as a fully-fledged witch, advised to help Guts in his quest against the inhumans.

"No matter how strong, for a human to fight a monster means
he has submerged his humanity and transformed himself into a greater monster."


-Schierke
